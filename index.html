4x4 Number Sliding Puzzle

Move the tiles by clicking on them to arrange them in order (1-15). (1-4)(5-8)(9-12)(13-15)
Use the **Shuffle** button to randomize the tiles in the drop down.
Solve the puzzle by placing all numbers in sequence with the empty space at the end by clicking the number next to the empty space.
